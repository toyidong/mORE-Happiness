Project 02

This project is from Scratch website.

To play, hover your mouse to right side of stage, click once to pick a tower, then place on the map. The starting coins are 400, you can only have 2 towers to start

The towers will point to mouse pointer and shoot automatically. After you destroy enemies, you earn coins to get more towers

Click towers on the map, you can choose to upgrade them with some coins or to sell them to get a portion of coins back

After each round, click start button to start next round
